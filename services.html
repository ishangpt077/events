Our

Services

Ishan Events is a bespoke wedding planner offering a full bouquet of wedding planning services that encompasses all aspects of putting together a wedding from conception all the way till the planning of the honeymoon, for wedding planning services in India as well as any exotic destination of your choice.


Turnkey Planning & Management

To make your day a memorable one make sure you don't let the biggest day of your life pass you by in a flurry of organization, negotiation, meetings and decisions. So, it's time to concentrate on making each moment count and soaking it all in. Sit back, relax and let us do all the work for you.

Destination & Venue Selection

We have the expertise to help you pick your dream destination and venue. Whether you want an exotic resort getaway for a beautiful beach ceremony or a fairytale setting or even aluxurious function in the heart of a metropolitan city - We have extensive destination locations for you.

Hospitality & Logistics

From the time we greet every one of your guests at the airport or their transportation we make sure that the Ishan Event's team is at your guests' service around the clock. From smooth arrivals and transport to seamless hotel check-ins, a 24-hour hospitality desk we pay constant attention to every detail.

Production of Decor And Designs

We at Ishan Events believe that extra touch can make all the difference to the look of a wedding event. Our panel of designers work closely with us, but we are completely open to working in tandem with any decor professionals. Our involvement can range from ideating on the look and feel to just supervising the execution of an external brief.

Catering Selection

The most important aspect for most of our weddings is the menu. Our team can be completely involved from the stage of choosing the right mix of vendors for the catering, planning the menus so that each meal at your event is different from the rest, and your guest experience a gastronomically satisfying.

Wedding Photography

Ishan Events believes that your wedding should be a self-spoken piece of art that tells a story of love for decades, so get your precious moments captured by us. We'll capture your special day and make it last forever.
